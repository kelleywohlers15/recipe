Baked Macaroni and cheese

Food Network Citation

Ingredients Directions
1(12 ounce) package Macaroni Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a 2-quart baking dish
1 egg In a large pot of salted water, lightly boil the macaroni for about 5 minutes until half cooked.
2 cups milk Wisk the egg and milk together in a large cup. Add butter and cheese to the egg and milk. Stir Well
2 tablespoons butter, melted Place the lightly cooked macaroni in the prepared baking dish.
2 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese Pour the egg and cheese liquid over the macaroni, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and stir well. Press the mixture evenly around the baking dish.
Salt and Pepper Bake uncovered, for 30 to 40 minutes, or until the top is brown.

Fettuccine Alfredo

All recipes Citation

Ingredients Directions
24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
1 cup butter Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3/4 pint heavy cream In a large saucepan, melt butter into cream over low heat. Add salt, pepper and garlic salt.
1 dash garlic salt Stir in cheese over medium heat until melted; this will thicken the sauce
3/4 cup grated Romano cheese Add pasta to sauce.
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese Use enough of the pasta so that all of the sauce is used and the pasta is thoroughly coated. Serve Immediately.

Spaghetti

Ingredients Directions
12 ounces dry spaghetti, uncooked Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
2 tablespoons canola oil Meanwhile, heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic; cook 5 minutes or until onion is tender, stirring frequently.
1 cup chopped onion Add beef; cook 7 minutes or until beef is crumbled and no longer pink, stirring occasionally. Drain.
2 tablespoons minced garlic Add pasta sauce to skillet; stir together.
1 pound ground round beef (85% lean) Simmer covered over medium-low heat 10 minutes or until hot.
1 can (24 oz each) Hunt's® Traditional Pasta Sauce Drain spaghetti. Serve meat sauce with spaghetti. Sprinkle with cheese, if desired.
